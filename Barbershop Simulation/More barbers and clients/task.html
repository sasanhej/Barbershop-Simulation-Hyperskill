Description

You have \(k\) barbers in our barbershop, clients may come at any time, and barbers may serve them for an arbitrary amount of time as well. We've asked the administrator to register each visitor's arrival time and ask at the exit how long they were served.

Using this data, restore the waiting time for each visitor.

Objectives

Write a program that takes the data on arrival and service times, computes the waiting time of every visitor, and prints them in separate lines.

Input format:

The first line contains the number of barbers \(k\) and the number of clients \(m\). In the next \(n\) lines, there are pairs \(a_i\), \(s_i\) separated by a space, where \(a_i\) and \(s_i\) are arrival times (in minutes, after 9 am) and the service time (in minutes) of i-th visitor.

Output format:

The output consists of \(n\) lines, where the \(i\)-th line contains the waiting time of the i-th client.
